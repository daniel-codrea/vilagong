Welcome to the website of Vila Gong!

 

Vila Gong is situated only 18 km from Cluj-Napoca and offers:

  • accommodation in 22 spacious, comfortable rooms for an optimal relaxation
  • high quality kitchen with classical meals or refined specialties
  • the possibility to organize conferences in an enjoyable ambiance
  • cultural or touristical programs for organized groups
  • free wireless internet access
  • parking places
  • laundry service

Touristical attractions:

  • the historical Cluj-Napoca
  • Gilău Mountains: lakes of "Gilău", "Someşul Cald", "Tarniţa" and "Beliş", village of Mărişel
  • Turda, Turda Salt Mine
  • Trascău Mountains: Turda Gorge, village of Rimetea
  • Călatei Valley (ethnographical region)
