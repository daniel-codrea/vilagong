1. Standard Rooms

Vila Gong has 4 standard rooms.

All the standard rooms have twin beds and are intended to accommodate one or two persons.

Facilities and services:

Bathroom with shower
Free WI-FI
Cable TV
Balcony (available in 2 rooms)
Free private parking
Payment by card
Restaurant
Garden, Terrace
Children’s playground
Laundry and ironing services (surcharge)

 

2. Superior rooms

Vila Gong has 14 superior rooms.

All the superior rooms have a comfortable double or twin bed and are intended to accommodate one or two persons.

Facilities and services:

Bathroom with shower
Free WI-FI
Cable TV
Refrigerator
Balcony (available in 10 rooms)
Free private parking
Payment by card
Restaurant
Garden, Terrace
Children’s playground
Laundry and ironing services (surcharge)

 

3. Triple rooms

Vila Gong has 4 triple rooms.

All the rooms have 3 separeted beds and are intended to accommodate three persons. We recommend these rooms for families with children.

Facilities and services:

Bathroom with shower
Free WI-FI
